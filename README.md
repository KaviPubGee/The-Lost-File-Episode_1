### Summary
The player is a "Temp Worker" hired for a late-night hotel occupancy check. What begins as a routine verification task quickly devolves into a ritualistic nightmare as the building's geometry begins to shift.

---

### The Narrative Sequence

#### **ACT I: The Routine**
*   **The Start:** Player stands in the elevator. The "Manager" (Boss) speaks through the intercom.
*   **Floor 4:** Knock on Room 402. *Result:* An angry guest shouts "Go away!"
*   **Floor 2 (Round 1):** Check Room 210. *Result:* Dead silence. Mark as "Unoccupied" on the clipboard.
*   **Floor 6:** Check Room 606. *Result:* Loud TV static inside; no answer.

#### **ACT II: The Glitch**
*   **The Return:** The Boss claims Room 210 called to complain about a "creep." Go back to apologize.
*   **The Shift:** The hallway is different—sickly green lights and distorted carpet. Room 210 has been replaced by a smooth, blank wall.
*   **The Radio:** Static grows. The Boss tells you to ignore it and proceed to Floor 10.

#### **ACT III: The Point of No Return**
*   **Floor 10:** The doors open to pitch blackness. A single phone rings at the end of the hall.
*   **The Realization:** A voice (not the Boss) answers: *"The sequence is complete. She will be waiting for you on 5. Don't look. Don't speak."*

#### **ACT IV: The Fifth Floor**
*   **The Guest:** The "Woman" (faceless, low-poly) enters the elevator.
*   **Gameplay:** The player must look at the floor and press the "1" button.
*   **The Ascent:** The elevator moves **UP** to the 10th floor instead of down. The music swells and the screen glitches.

---

### 🎲 ACT V: The Coin Toss (Endings)
The final outcome is determined by a **50/50 random chance** upon reaching the 10th floor.
